Berthing Operation and Harbor Facilities

The Heavy Duty Quayside spans 210 meters in length and 30–50 meters in width, with a ground load capacity of 30 tonnes per square meter and a total area of approximately 10,329 square meters. It is equipped with eighteen 70 t mooring bollards.

Hot work operations (welding and assembly), blasting and painting (indoor and outdoor) are supported, enabling vessel maintenance and mobilization/demobilization activities.

The elevation from the quayside to mean sea level (MSL) ranges from approximately 1.33 to 2.5 meters. With small tidal variations and favorable year-round weather conditions, the site offers safe storage and reduces risks associated with long-distance land transport, making it an ideal base for maritime engineering operations. One-stop maintenance and support services are available.

Site Facilities

1.Workshop: Equipped with 50/150/250-tonne gantry cranes with a maximum lifting height of 36 meters, as well as forklifts and roller stand.

2.Storage Area: Over 10.5 hectares of outdoor storage space available.

3.Office Area: Suitable for short-, medium-, or long-term operations, with air-conditioning, supervisors' offices, meeting rooms, and drinking water facilities.
