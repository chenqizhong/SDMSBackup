History

Sing Da Marine Structure Co., Ltd. (SDMS), established by China Steel Corporation (CSC) on April 19, 2018, has successfully delivered Taiwan’s first 100% locally offshore jacket foundation, gaining a head start in the wind power industry.

Building on its established expertise, SDMS has strategically expanded its steel structure manufacturing and coating technologies to meet evolving industry demands and reinforce its position in Taiwan’s green energy and infrastructure markets.

Vision & Mission

Guided by the CSC Group’s core values of “teamwork, entrepreneurial approach, down-to-earthness, and pursuit of innovation,” SDMS remains committed to advancing its technological and operational excellence. Through the integration of robust supply chain management capabilities, SDMS continues to strengthen its role in elevating the industrial value chain while reinforcing its long-term commitment to sustainable growth and value creation.

Feature

SDMS has introduced offshore wind manufacturing technologies and expertise from Europe while independently developing key jacket foundation technologies. In response to market dynamics, SDMS has also diversified into advanced steel structure fabrication and established robust technical service capabilities. It continues to expand across structural steel, coating, technical personnel services, berthing, and wind power-related operations, enhancing industrial value and strengthening regional competitiveness.
