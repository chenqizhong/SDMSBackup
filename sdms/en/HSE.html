Sing Da Marine Structure Corporation (SDMS)
Environmental, Health, and Safety (EHS) Policy

SDMS is committed to fulfilling its responsibilities in occupational safety, health, and environmental protection. In support of global energy conservation, carbon reduction, and national offshore wind power development, we are dedicated to sustainable corporate operations. We promote an EHS management system and actively adopt appropriate technologies and management tools to reduce operational risks and implement effective environmental protection measures. We are committed to:

  1. Safeguarding worker safety and eliminating potential risks.
  2. Enhancing hygiene management and promoting employee health.
  3. Advancing offshore wind power and protecting the ecological environment.
  4. Complying with environmental regulations and implementing pollution prevention.
  5. Promoting energy saving and carbon reduction for long-term sustainability.

Occupational Health and Safety Goals

The primary objective is to ensure that all operations conducted and managed by SDMS are carried out in a manner that safeguards the safety and health of personnel. This means that occupational health and safety protection standards are of paramount importance throughout the entire project duration. The ultimate goal is zero harm to all personnel involved in the project and to those who may be affected by the operations.

Environmental Goals

The primary objective is to prevent environmental pollution and ensure that all operations conducted and managed by SDMS comply with environmental protection regulations throughout the production period. The ultimate goal is to eliminate any potential environmental pollution caused by our activities.
